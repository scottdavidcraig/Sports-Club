About the club

Livingston Badminton Club was originally founded in 2003 as an after school club for children. Soon afterwards an adult club was formed, initially as a social club. The adult and junior sections were subsequently split, and over the years the adult club has gradually developed into a competitive standard badminton club.

The club is fully affiliated with Badminton Scotland and currently has four teams competing into the Lothian Leagues. We play level and mixed doubles across four courts, chosen via racket selection, in a competitive yet friendly environment. We use feather shuttlecocks for all of our matches.

Teams and leagues

The club currently has two gents doubles teams, a ladies doubles team and a mixed doubles team competing in the Lothian Leagues. Find out how we are getting on here

Membership

We are always eager to welcome new members - anyone is welcome to come along and try a club night for free.

If you enjoy your first club night and would like to join the club then the weekly fee will be £7.50 (payable to reception). Every October an annual fee of £36 is due, and after payment of this the weekly fee reduces to £6.00.

However, the standard of play at the club is not suitable for complete beginners. If you are new to the sport of badminton we would still fully encourage you to try out the sport and coaching is available from the following places:
