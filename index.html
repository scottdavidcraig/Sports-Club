About the club

We are a competitive standard club with two gents doubles teams, a ladies doubles team and a mixed doubles team competing in the Lothian Leagues.

We play level and mixed doubles across four courts, chosen via racket selection, in a competitive yet friendly environment. We use feather shuttlecocks for all of our matches

Join us

We are always eager to welcome new members and anyone is welcome to come along and try a session for free. However, the standard of play at the club is not suitable for complete beginners.

If you enjoy your first session and would like to join the club there is a weekly cost of £7.50, dropping to £6 after payment of the annual membership fee of £36 in October.

If you are new to the sport of badminton we would still fully encourage you to try out the sport. Coaching is available from:

Edinburgh Badminton Academy
