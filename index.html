    About the Club

    We are a competitive standard club with teams competing in the Lothian Leagues 

    We play level and mixed doubles across 4/6 courts, courts are graded in terms of ability with maximum of 6 on each court, in a competitive yet friendly environment. We use quality Yonex / Yehlex feather shuttlecocks for all of our sessions and matches.

     £8 per session 

    Membership

    £8 per session or £25 monthly standing order

    We are always eager to welcome new members and anyone is welcome to come along and try a session for free. However, the standard of play at the club is not suitable for complete beginners. See the Facebook page for more details of each session.
